Goodbye Dinosaurmod

Updated 11th November 2023 (Central European Time)

R.I.P Dinosaurmod

What DinosaurMod Is

DinosaurMod was supposed to be a mod of Penguinmod

where you can have much more extensions and new blocks

Including Menus and the CHAT-GPT Extension

Why DinosaurMod Shut down

DinosaurMod was recently having alot of bugs

examples: (only showing readme.md file), (blocks clumped together)

and (infinitely loading), since there was only one person editing

and was confused and had no idea how to fix the (infinitely loading) bug.

there was only one choice left, due to barely anyone working on dinosaurmod.
